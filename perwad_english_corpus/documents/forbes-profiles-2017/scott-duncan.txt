
			Scott Duncan and his three older siblings inherited a stake in their late father Dan Duncan's pipeline firm Enterprise Products Partners in 2010. The company owns natural gas processing plants, oil and gas storage facilities and 49,000 miles of pipelines.  Like his two siblings Dannine Avara and Milane Frantz, Duncan is not actively involved in the business.Their sister Randa Duncan Williams serves as non-executive chairman of the board. Scott Duncan is one of the youngest billionaires in America.
