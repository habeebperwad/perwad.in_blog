
			Igor Altushkin is the founder and largest shareholder of the Russian Copper Company (RMK), Russia's third-largest copper producer. He started his career as a scrap metal trader in the early 1990s. Altushkin, who has six children, is a key supporter of the Russian Orthodox Church and owns one of Madonna's former homes in London.  A big fan of boxing and target shooting, his RMK co-founded promotion company RCC Boxing Promotions in November 2016 to organize and hold bouts in combat sport and boxing.
