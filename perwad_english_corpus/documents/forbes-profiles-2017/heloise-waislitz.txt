
			Thorney Investments, built with husband Alex Waislitz (they've since separated), holds stakes in more than 20 listed companies, ranging from gas and mining services to renewable energy, biotech and airline bookings.  Also owns about 30% of listed investment company Thorney Opportunities and a property portfolio. Sister of billionaires Anthony Pratt  and Fiona Geminder, the three siblings hold stakes in the Melbourne family's Visy Asia-Pacific packaging business, founded by the late Richard Pratt. She oversees the Pratt Foundation's $1 billion fund, which distributes around $20 million annually.
