
			Clelia Haji-Ioannou is a billionaire thanks to her stake in cut-price carrier easyJet. Her brother, Stelios, founded the budget airline in 1995. Born in Athens, Clelia's father Loucas Haji-Ioannou was the self-styled "King of Tankers" and ran the family's shipping company.  Clelia owns more than $40 million in real estate in Monaco, Cyprus, Athens and London. She sits on the board of the Cyprus Marine Environment Protection Association.
