
				
					Suzuki is President and CEO of cosmetics firm Pola Orbis.
				
				
					He is the adopted son of the company's founder, Tsuneshi Suzuki.
				
				
					Satoshi battled Tsuneshi's estranged wife for the inheritance in 2000 and won.
				
				
					He has expanded the Tokyo-listed beauty products company with a focus on natural and organic cosmetics. 
				
				
					His father established the Pola Museum of Art in Hakone, Japan, with works from Impressionists and post-Impressionists like Monet and Picasso.
