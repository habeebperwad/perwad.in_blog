
				
					Kommer's father and uncle, Jan and Rien, founded Damen Shipyards in a shed next to the family home in the Dutch town of Hardinxveld-Giessendam in 1922.
				
				
					Kommer bought the shipyard in 1969 and introduced the modular shipbuilding concept to the construction of small boats and launches.
				
				
					Damen Group is now one of Europe's largest family-owned shipbuilders, generating $1.7 billion in annual revenue worldwide in 2016.
