
				
					Liu Zhongtian leads Hong Kong-listed aluminum manufacturer China Zhongwang Holdings. 
				
				
					In September 2016, Liu denied reports that his company might be dodging U.S. tariffs by storing  a reported 6% of the world's aluminum in Mexico. 
				
				
					That followed news in late August 2016 that Zhongwang USA LLC, which is owned by Liu, agreed to buy aluminum processor Aleris Corp. of Cleveland for $1.1 billion.
