
			Karen Pritzker inherited a portion of the Chicago-based Pritzker family fortune and, with her husband Michael Vlock, has invested in public companies like Apple as well as private biotech and medical device firms.  Her grandfather A.N. Pritzker (d. 1986) built industrial conglomerate Marmon and hotel chain Hyatt with sons Jay (d. 1999), Donald (d. 1972) and Robert (d. 2011), Karen's father. The couple has donated to Yale, Teach for America and the Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson's Research. Pritzker and daughter Dana Schwartz have partnered with Robert Redford's son Jamie to form documentary studio KPJR Films; Pritzker produced three of Jamie Redford's films.
