
			Poland's "King of Shoes" and chairman of CCC, the country's largest footwear retailer, Dariusz Milek fell off the Forbes billionaires list in 2016 but rejoins in 2017 following a jump in the share price of CCC.  The company, which expanded into Serbia and Russia in 2016, sells more than 40 million pairs of shoes annually from over 1,000 stores in 17 countries. Milek is also investing in technology, specifically 3-D printing. An avid sporstman, he sponsors the CCC Polkowice women's basketball team and the cycling team CCC Sprandi.
