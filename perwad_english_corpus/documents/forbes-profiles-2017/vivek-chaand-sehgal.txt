
			Wealth of Vivek Chaand Sehgal, cofounder of the $7.2 billion (revenue) Samvardhana Motherson Group got a 41.6 billion boost as profits at auto-components flagship Motherson Sumi Systems rose 38% in the year ended March 2016, giving a fillip to its shares.  Japanese partner Sumitomo owns more than a fourth of Motherson Sumi, which has 145 factories in 25 countries and gets 85% of its $5.7 billion revenue from overseas. Continuing its strategy of growing through acquisitions, the company recently agreed to pay $620 million to acquire Finland's PKC Group, an auto parts supplier. He started the group in 1975 as a silver trading outfit along with his mother, hence the name Motherson.
