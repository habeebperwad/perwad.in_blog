
			San Francisco developer Jay Paul builds and leases high-end office space to tech bigwigs, including Hewlett-Packard, Amazon and Microsoft. His privately held Jay Paul Co. has built 8 million square feet of office space in Silicon Valley, and has 6 million more in the pipeline.  It's constructing vast campuses in Sunnyvale, Calif. for Apple and Google. The latter firm already leases more than a million square feet of office space from Jay Paul Co. Paul's first project in San Francisco, a 56-story tower with offices and luxury condos, is slated to open in 2017. He is the grandson of Italian immigrants and owns two yachts.
