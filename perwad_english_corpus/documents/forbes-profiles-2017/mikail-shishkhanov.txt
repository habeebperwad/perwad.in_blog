
			Mikail Shishkhanov started working with the BIN Group, founded by his uncle, billionaire Mikhail Gutseriev, in 1992 when he was a student at People's Friendship University of Russia. Two years later, he moved to its financial subsidiary, Binbank, and today is its chairman.  He also oversees the construction companies under the BIN Group umbrella. Under his leadership, the conglomerate made multiple deals in the financial sector in 2015, including acquiring MDM Bank from billionaire Sergei Popov and the Raiffeisen Pension Fund. In 2016 BIN Group was renamed as Safmar Group. The company is named after his grandparents Safarbek and Marem.
