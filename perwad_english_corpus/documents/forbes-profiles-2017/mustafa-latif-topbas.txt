
			Mustafa Latif Topbas is CEO of BIM, Turkey's largest chain of discount stores. As of early 2017, it had 5,690 outlets in Turkey, 329 in Morocco and 215 in Egypt. Topbas owns almost 15% of BIM, which trades on the Istanbul Stock Exchange, and also has investments in Ulker Group food companies (source of billionaire Murat Ulker's wealth), plus an interest in a textile manufacturer.  His daughter, Fatma, is married to Ali Ulker, the nephew of Murat Ulker. Mustafa Latif Topbas, who doesn't give interviews, has a longstanding friendship with Turkey's president, Recep Tayyip Erdogan, and his family. He co-founded BIM in 1995.
