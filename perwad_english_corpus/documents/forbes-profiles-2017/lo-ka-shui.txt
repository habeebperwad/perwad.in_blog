
			Lo Ka Shui chairs Great Eagle and also controls a commercial property REIT, Champion, and a hotel REIT, Langham Hospitality Investments. The group's luxury hotel chain, under the Langham and Langham Place brands, boasts more than 20 locations worldwide.  Elder daughter Katherine Lo works for the group in the U.S.; elder son Alexander Lo in Hong Kong. Brother Vincent Lo is also a real estate billionaire. In 1980 Lo was practicing as a cardiologist in the U.S. when his father called him back to help with the family business.
