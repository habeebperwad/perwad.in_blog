
			After 28 years at the helm, Helmut Sohmen retired in 2014 as chairman of the family's BW Group and listed BW Offshore. Married to the daughter of late shipping tycoon Pao Yue-Kong, the Austrian citizen has lived in Hong Kong for decades.  A Southern Methodist University law school alumnus, he supports a graduate scholarship program for Chinese lawyers studying at SMU's Dedman School of Law. Sohmen's Oxford- and Harvard-educated eldest son, Andreas Sohmen-Pao, now runs the group.
