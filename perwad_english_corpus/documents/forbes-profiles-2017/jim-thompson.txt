
			Jim Thompson started his moving company, Crown Worldwide, in Japan in 1965, with $1,000 at the behest of co-workers after their former employer closed the business. He moved its headquarters to Hong Kong in 1978, just before China kicked off its boom.  It is now one of the world's largest privately held relocation companies, and it's also involved in document storage and fine arts transportation, with operations in 60 countries. A native of the U.S. state of New Jersey, he owns a country estate in Ireland, near the home of his ancestors.
