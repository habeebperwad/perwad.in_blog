
			Tamara Gustavson is the daughter of B. Wayne Hughes, the cofounder of self-storage giant Public Storage. Gustavson joined the company in 1983 and rose to vice president before leaving in 2003 to raise her children.  She returned four years later as a board member and remains Public Storage's largest individual shareholder. In August 2016 Gustavson joined the board of another of her father's companies, American Homes 4 Rent, a publicly-traded REIT focusing on single-family rental homes. She often donates to the HollyRod Foundation, which researches autism, Parkinson's and other diseases. Both her father and brother, B. Wayne Hughes Jr., are also billionaires.
