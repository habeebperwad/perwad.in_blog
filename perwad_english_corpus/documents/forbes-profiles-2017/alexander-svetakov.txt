
			Alexander Svetakov's holding company, Absolut Group, has interests in real estate, trading and insurance. As a student at the Moscow Institute of Electronic Technology in the early 1990s, he and his classmates ran a wholesale business, importing home appliances and electronics from Singapore.  Later he invested in banking, and eventually sold Absolut Bank for nearly $1 billion. Since 2010, Absolut Group has invested in residential and commercial property development.
