
			Jim Pattison presides over a massive conglomerate, the Jim Pattison Group, that stems from a single GM dealership in 1961. More than 200 acquisitions later, his Jim Pattison Group still sells cars but is also active in media, forestry products, packaging, and food and beverages.  In total, the $9.6 billion (revenues) empire has 24 divisions employing 42,000 people. His entertainment division includes Guinness World Records and the Ripley's Believe It Or Not! chain. Pattison also has significant stakes in forest products company Canfor and natural gas retailer Just Energy. An octogenarian, Pattison still goes to work every day. He owns a Palm Springs home that once belonged to Frank Sinatra and has a vintage car collection.
