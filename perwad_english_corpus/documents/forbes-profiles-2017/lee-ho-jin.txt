
			Lee Ho-Jin is the youngest son of Lee Eam-Yung, the founder of Taekwang Industrial. Starting out in the 1950s, the elder Lee built Taekwang Industries into avertically integrated manufacturer that became synonymous with Korea's textile and petrochemical prowess.  Ho-Jin is known mostly for successfully turning the core industrial business he inherited from his father into a diversified group with financial, media and leisure components. He is still battling liver cancer.
