
			Peter Sondakh owns a stake in taxi company Express Transindo Utama, which has faced competition from online apps like Uber that let people hail taxis using a smartphone. Sondakh also owns property. In May 2016 he sold the Surfers Paradise Marriott Resort & Spa in Queensland.  His new hotels include the Langkawi International Convention Centre and the St. Regis Langkawi, which opened at the Malaysian resort in April 2016, and the $500 million Capital Place mixed-use property in Jakarta, which opened in June 2016. In 2007 Sondakh sold his stake in cell phone company Excelcomindo Pratama for more than $400 million.
