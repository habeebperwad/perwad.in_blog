
				
					Arison is chairman of the world's largest cruise ship operator, Carnival Corp.
				
				
					He spent 34 years as CEO of Carnival, which his father cofounded, before stepping down in June 2013.
				
				
					While CEO, he helped grow Carnival's fleet from two ships to more than 100.
				
				
					In 1995 Arison took control of the Miami Heat, the NBA franchise his father helped bring to Florida.
