
			Lee Hwa-Kyung is the second daughter of Dongyang Group founder Lee Yang-Koo. She began her career in Donyang's confectionary business, now known as Orion Confectionary, in 1975. Orion's portfolio now includes more than 100 brands in such categories as biscuits, caramel, chewing gum and snacks.  The company's most famous product is Choco Pie, consisting of a combination of biscuit, chocolate and marshmallow filling. Sales for her Orion are booming in China: the country generates twice as much revenue for the maker of Choco Pies as South Korea. Her husband Dam Chul-Gon serves as chairman.
