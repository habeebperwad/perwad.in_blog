
			Hong Seok-Joh heads Korea's largest convenience store company, BGF Retail. Operating under the brand name CU, it has more than 8,000 stores and generated revenue of over $30 billion. The business got its start in 1989 via a franchise licensing agreement between the Hong family's Bokwang Group and Japanese convenience store operator FamilyMart.  His sister Hong Ra-Hee, is director of Leeum Samsung Museum of Art and the wife of Samsung Group Chairman Lee Kun-Hee, South Korea's richest person.
