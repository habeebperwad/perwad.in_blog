
				
					Adikoesoemo founded AKR Corporindo as chemicals trader in 1960 and listed it on the Indonesian Stock Exchange in 1994. 
				
				
					 AKR Corporindo is now engaged in petroleum trading and distribution, logistics services and chemical manufacturing.
				
				
					In November 2016, AKR Corporindo and British oil and gas firm BP announced plans to launch an aviation fuel joint venture.
