
			Edwin Leong is the sixth son of Henry G. Leong, a comprador of British trading house Jardine Matheson. Just nine years old when his father died, Edwin went on to build a fortune of his own. His investment firm, Tai Hung Fai Enterprise, specializes in residential, commercial and industrial properties.  His foreign partners include Oakwood, an apartment rental company in Los Angeles, Thailand-based Onyx Hospitality, and Inter-Continental Hotels. His Tai Hung Fai Charitable Foundation focuses on ways to help needy children and the elderly.
