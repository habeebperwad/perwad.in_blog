
			Shamsheer Vayalil, son-in-law of Gulf retailing billionaire M.A. Yusuff Ali, founded and runs VPS Healthcare, a network of hospitals, clinics and pharmacies in the UAE and India. Born into a business family in the southern Indian state of Kerala, he migrated to the Middle East after medical studies and worked as a radiologist at a local hospital.  Backed initially by his father-in-law, he started with one hospital in Abu Dhabi in 2007. VPS expanded into India last year by acquiring the Rockland Hospital chain in Delhi and the Lakeshore Hospital in Kochi. VPS is investing in Azerbaijan where it plans to build a factory to make pharmaceuticals. Vayalil is a fitness freak and marathon runner.
