
			Son of a jewelry store owner, Joy Alukkas dropped out of school to go to the Middle East to open the family's first overseas store in 1987 in Abu Dhabi. He broke off to launch his own Joyalukkas brand which now has 130 outlets spread across India and 13 other countries in the Middle East, South East Asia as well as the U. K. and the U.S. Other interests include an expanding money exchange, luxury aviation, malls, silks and, real estate. His son John Paul works with him.
