
				
					Lin is chairman of Chang Chun Group, one of Asia's largest petrochemical companies with plants in Taiwan, China and Southeast Asia.
				
				
					He cofounded the group with Tseng Shin-yi and the late M.K. Liao. It now has $9 billion in sales and more than 10,000 employees
				
				
					The name "Chang Chun" in Chinese loosely means "long spring," and reflects the wish among the three that their bonds would endure.
