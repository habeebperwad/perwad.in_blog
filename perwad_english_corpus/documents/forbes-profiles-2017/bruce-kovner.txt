
			Macro-investing legend Bruce Kovner no longer runs his hedge fund firm, Caxton Associates, but still benefits as an investor. Still, the majority of his fortune today is in his family office, CAM Capital.  Born in Brooklyn, he attended Harvard on a scholarship. Once a cab driver in New York City, Kovner took classes at the Juilliard School, where he now serves as chairman. Kovner also serves as a director for the Metropolitan Opera. He is a conservative and longtime backer of the American Enterprise Institute.
