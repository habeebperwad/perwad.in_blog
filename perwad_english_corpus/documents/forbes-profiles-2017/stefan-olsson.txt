
			Stefan Olsson is a part owner of Stena Sphere, a Swedish conglomerate with interests in shipping, offshore drilling, property and metal recycling, founded by his father in 1939. The company is helmed by his older brother, Dan Sten Olsson (also a billionaire), who owns 51% of the company, more than double Stefan's 24.5% stake.  Stefan, a Catholic priest, lives in London.
