
				
					Wei and his three brothers--Ing-Chou, Yin-Chun and Yin-Heng--founded Cayman Islands-based Tingyi. 
				
				
					Tingyi is now a big producer of ready-to-drink tea and bottled water in China; it also supplies half of the country's instant noodle market. 
				
				
					The family has an investment in Taipei 101, Taiwan's tallest building, and the Dicos fried chicken chain.
