
			Self-made property tycoon Li Sze Lim expanded his Guangzhou R&F Properties to 28 cities--from Johor, Malaysia to San Jose, California--as well as going deeper into China. He's also diversifying into hotels in partnership with brands such as Marriott, Hyatt and Hilton, and quickly expanding into shopping malls and offices.  He cofounded R&F Properties in 1994 with Zhang Li on the mainland.
