
			Cofounder of Infosys Senapathy "Kris" Gopalakrishnan is backing startups after retiring from the company in 2014. He has personally invested in close to 50 startups, including 15 companies through his business incubator Axilor Ventures.  He has also funded and created Itihaasa, a digital app that chronicles the evolution of India's IT industry through, among much else, interviews with industry stalwarts such as Azim Premji and fellow Infosys cofounder N.R. Narayana Murthy. He recently pledged $2.5 million for a new centre for innovation and entrepreneurship at IIT Madras, his alma mater.
