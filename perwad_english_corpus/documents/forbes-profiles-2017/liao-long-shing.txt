
				
					Liao inherited his stake in Chang Chun Group from his late father, M.K. Liao, one of three founders. 
				
				
					Chang Chun Group is now one of Asia's largest petrochemical companies with plants in Taiwan, mainland China, Malaysia, Indonesia and Singapore.
				
				
					The name "Chang Chun" in Chinese loosely means "long spring," and reflects the wish among the three that their bonds would endure.
