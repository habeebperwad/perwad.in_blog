
				
					Chen Liying cofounded Yunda Express, one of China's biggest logistics companies, in Shanghai in 1999. 
				
				
					Yunda finished backdoor listing at the end of Dec. 2016 and changed the Shenzhen-listed target company name from Ningbo Xinhai Electric to Yunda Holding. 

				
				
					Chen owns near 21% of the listed company Yunda Holding, and serves as vice chairman.

				
				
					 Husband Nie Tengyun is a fellow billionaire and chairman of Yunda Holding.
