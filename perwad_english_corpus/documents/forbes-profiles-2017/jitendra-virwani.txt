
			Fast-rising property baron Jitendra Virwani's Embassy Group, is Bangalore's biggest developer of sprawling office parks with partners such as private equity firms Blackstone and Warburg Pincus. Embassy has a roster of multinational clients such as Wells Fargo, Yahoo and Burberry, as well as homegrown e-retailer Flipkart.  The developer's upcoming project in its home city is Embassy Springs, a 300- acre township near Bangalore airport. Next up is a REIT listing of Embassy's office assets. Son Karan, who works with him, is overseeing a new joint venture in shared office spaces with WeWork. Passionate about horses, Virwani owns a riding school.
