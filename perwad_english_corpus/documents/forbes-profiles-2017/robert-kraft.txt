
			Kraft keeps expanding his sports empire. He bought a stake in mixed martial arts promoter Ultimate Fighting Championship when the Fertitta brothers, also billionaires, sold it for $4 billion in August. He purchased the New England Patriots for $172 million in 1994 with profits from his paper and packaging conglomerate; the team is now worth $3.4 billion.  He also owns the New England Revolution MLS club.
