
			Danil Khachaturov studied construction and finance at schools in Moscow. He then  went to work at privately held BIN Bank and later oil company Slavneft, led at the time by billionaire Mikhail Gutseriev.  With partners, he started buying up then-insolvent Rosgosstrakh from the government back in 2001. Today he has a controlling stake in the $2.4 billion (revenue) company, which is Russia's largest insurance business.
