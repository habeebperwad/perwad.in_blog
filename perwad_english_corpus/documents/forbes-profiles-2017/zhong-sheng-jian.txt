
			Zhong Sheng Jian chairs Yanlord Land, one of the largest non-government-owned real estate developers in China.  Amid worries about slowing economic growth there, Yanlord has of late invested in land and assets in major cities including Nanjing, Shanghai and Tianjin.  Its prominent China partners include Ping An Insurance, China Merchants Property Development and Poly Real Estate Group.  Zhong, a mainland native, moved to Singapore in 1988.
