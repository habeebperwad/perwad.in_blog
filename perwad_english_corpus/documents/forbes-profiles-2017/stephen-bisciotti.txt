
			Baltimore Ravens owner Steve Bisciotti cofounded one of the world's largest private staffing firms, Allegis Group, with his cousin Jim Davis. After being raised by a single mother in Baltimore, Bisciotti attended nearby Salisbury State University, where he majored in liberal arts.  Upon graduating, he spent a year working in the temporary help industry before cofounding Allegis, which now has 15,000 internal employees, 120,000 contracted workers and offices on four continents. Outside of the workplace, Bisciotti enjoys golfing and boating and serves on the board of Catholic Charities.
