
			Michael Moritz, born in Wales, is a partner at Silicon Valley venture capital firm Sequoia Capital. He handed over control of the firm in 2012 due to an incurable, undisclosed illness. Over the years he has invested in some of Silicon Valley's most successful tech companies, including Google, LinkedIn and Yahoo.  A former journalist, Moritz wrote an influential biography of Steve Jobs in 1984.  He was knighted by Queen Elizabeth for his investing success and charitable giving.
