
				
					The oligarch's largest holding today is his stake in iron ore and steel giant Metalloinvest.
				
				
					An early Facebook investor, he currently owns stakes in JD.com and Xiaomi.
				
				
					He also owns shares in telecom, mining and media companies, as well as the Arsenal Football Club.
				
				
					The Uzbekistan-born tycoon made his first fortune more than two decades ago making plastic bags, a commodity so scarce in the former Soviet Union that people washed and reused them.
				
				
					He headed Gazprom Investholding, a subsidiary of state-run conglomerate Gazprom, from 2000 to 2014.
