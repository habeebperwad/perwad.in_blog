
			With partners David Kabiller and John Liew, Cliff Asness founded AQR as a hedge fund in 1998. It has become possibly the fastest-growing money manager on earth, with $185 billion of assets under management.  A Goldman Sachs veteran, Asness backed lawmakers who supported New York's marriage equality initiative. He is also a board member of the International Rescue Committee,  which works to support refugees around the world, and supports biomedical research at the Broad Institute at MIT and Harvard and the Tisch Brain Tumor Center at Duke University.
