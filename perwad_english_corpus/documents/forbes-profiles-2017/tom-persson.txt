
			Tom Persson is third generation of the family that made its fortune from global cheap chic retailer H&M. His grandfather, Erling Persson, founded the chain in 1947. His brother Karl-Johan is CEO and his father, Sweden's richest person Stefan Persson, is chairman.  Tom is a graduate of the Met Film School in London. He lives in Stockholm and works in movie production. His father, brother, sister Charlotte Söderström, and aunt Lottie Tham are all billionaires.
