
			After stepping down from the board of Indian IT giant Infosys six years ago, cofounder K. Dinesh, along with wife Asha, has devoted himself to philanthropy. They have funded a cancer center at the Narayana Health Hospital and a clinic, both on their ancestral land in Mysore city.  His wife and two daughters have launched VedaEarth, a brand of herbal personal care products that combine ayurveda and aromatherapy.
