
				
					Zong Qinghou turned his privately-owned Hangzhou Wahaha Group into one of China's largest homegrown seller of bottled water, teas and milk drinks. 

				
				
					Zong founded Hangzhou Wahaha as a store in a children's school in the city of Hangzhou in 1987. 
				
				
					Zong is an outspoken advocate of lower taxes to support economic growth in China.
				
				
					Daughter Kelly is heir apparent.
