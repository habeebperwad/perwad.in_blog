
			Entrepreneur He Xiangjian built Midea Group into one of China's largest appliance makers, then stepped down from operations in 2012. In June 2016 Midea bought 80% of Toshiba's money-losing home appliance arm for $500 million.  In May 2016, the company bid about $5 billion for German industrial robot firm Kuka. The deal initially drew some concern about the transfer of advanced technology to China, but was approved and closed in January 2017.
