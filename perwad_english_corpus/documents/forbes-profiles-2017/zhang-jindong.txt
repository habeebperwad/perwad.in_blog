
				
					Zhang Jindong chairs Suning Commerce Group, one of China's largest appliance retailers.
				
				
					In June 2016 his private firm Suning Holdings acquired 70% of soccer club F.C. Inter Milan for $300 million; Suning Holdings also owns 61% of online broadcaster PPTV.
				
				
					Jindong's brother Zhang Guiping is also a billionaire.
