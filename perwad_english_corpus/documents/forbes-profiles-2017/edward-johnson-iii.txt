
				
					After a stint in the Army, Ned joined Fidelity, which his dad founded in 1946, as an analyst. 
				
				
					Ned led Fidelity as CEO for 37 years; his daughter Abigail replaced him in 2014.
				
				
					He ran the famed Magellan Fund before promoting buy-what-you-know manager Peter Lynch to run it.  
				
				
					He's an art collector and frequently loans pieces to various museums throughout New England.
