
			Randa Duncan Williams, a former lawyer, is non-executive chairman of Enterprise Products Partners, the pipeline giant her father Dan Duncan (d. 2010) founded in 1968. Williams is the only one of the four siblings involved in running the family business, which owns 49,000 miles of energy pipelines, as well as natural gas processing plants and storage facilities.  The Rice University alumna also serves on her alma mater's board of trustees. She is actively involved in the University of Texas Health Science Center's Children's Learning Institute, which has a clinic named after her father.
