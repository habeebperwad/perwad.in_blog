
				
					Chinese-Thai businessman Chanchai Ruayrungruang, also known as Yan Bin, leads the privately held Reignwood Group. 

				
				
					Reignwood's best-known assets are Reignwood Centre -- an office, hotel and retail complex in central Beijing, and the Pine Valley golf course in Beijing. 
				
				
					It also has exclusive production rights for the Red Bull energy drink in China. 
				
				
					Chanchai started the business in Thailand in 1984 and moved into China a dozen years later.
