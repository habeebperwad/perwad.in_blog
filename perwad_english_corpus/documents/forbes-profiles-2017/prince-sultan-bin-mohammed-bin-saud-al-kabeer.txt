
			Prince Sultan Al Kabeer is a member of the Saudi royal family. He founded dairy company Almarai in 1977 and took it public in 2005, keeping a nearly 24% stake. One of the largest dairy operations in the Middle East, Almarai transports milk from its Holstein cows throughout the Gulf countries.  According to the Breeders' Cup horse racing organization, Al Kabeer keeps a stud farm outside of Riyadh where he trains some 100 horses.
