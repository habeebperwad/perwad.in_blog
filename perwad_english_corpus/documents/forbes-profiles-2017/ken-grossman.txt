
				
					In 1980, his Sierra Nevada Brewing brewed its first batch of what is now its most popular beer, Sierra Nevada Pale Ale.

				
				
					Now one of America's premier craft breweries, sales are more than $300 million. 
				
				
					More than 10,000 solar panels are installed at the original Chico, CA brewery, and 99.8% of the facility's waste is recycled or composted. 
				
				
					Grossman's son runs a new brewery in North Carolina.
