
			Lesley Bamberger is the owner of Kroonenberg Groep, one of the largest privately-held real estate companies in the Netherlands. It has a portfolio of retail, industrial and residential buildings in Netherlands and the United States.  His grandfather, Jacob Kroonenberg, founded the company. Bamberger joined in 1986 and inherited it in 1996 after his grandfather's death. He divides his time between Amsterdam and New York City.
