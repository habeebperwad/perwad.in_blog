
				
					Maezawa founded Japan's largest online fashion mall, Zozotown.
				
				
					Zozotown's parent firm, Start Today, is listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange; Maezawa owns 39%. 
				
				
					Maezawa got his start selling CDs and records of his favorite bands by mail from his home. 
				
				
					He collects modern art and plans to build a museum in Chiba, east of Tokyo. 
				
				
					In 2016 he purchased paintings by Jean-Michel Basquiat and Pablo Picasso, spending about $80 million.
