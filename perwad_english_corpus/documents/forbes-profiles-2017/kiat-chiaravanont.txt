
			Kiat Chiaravanont who lives in Hong Kong, is a cousin of Thailand's second richest man Dhanin Chearavanont. His 5.76% stake in CP Group, the holding outfit that owns shares in the Charoen Pokphand conglomerate, is his biggest asset.  After dropping off last year, Kiat regains his spot in the ranks on new information about CP Group's private holdings. Kiat is the eldest son of Choncharoen, Dhanin's uncle.
