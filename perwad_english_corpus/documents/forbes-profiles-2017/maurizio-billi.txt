
			Maurizio Billi is president of Eurofarma, a Brazil-based pharmaceutical giant that sells generic versions of drugs such as Viagra. The firm was founded by his father in 1972 and is now the third largest pharmaceutical company in Brazil, boasting the largest sales force in Brazil.  In 2009 Eurofarma first extended its reach beyond Brazil, buying Argentina-based Quesada company, which sold products focused in gastroenterology and cardiology. In the following years following it acquired medical suppliers and labs throughout Central and South America. An avid racer, Billi won Brazil's Porsche Master Cup in 2013. His Eurofarma team has won the Brazilian circuit of Stock Car Brasil, the local equivalent of the Nascar, five times.
