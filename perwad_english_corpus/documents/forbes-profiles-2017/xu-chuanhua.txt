
				
					Xu Chuanhua took the plunge as an entrepreneur in 1986 at age 52 as a supplier of liquid soap and detergent. 

				
				
					Xu is out of daily operations; his sons Guanju and Guanbao are chairman and vice chairman of Transfar Group.
				
				
					Today, his Transfar Group generates most of its business in logistics and chemicals.
