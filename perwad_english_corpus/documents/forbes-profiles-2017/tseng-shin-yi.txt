
				
					Tseng cofounded Chang Chun Group in Taiwan with Suhon Lin, the current chairman, and the late M.K. Liao. 
				
				
					Chang Chun Group is now one of Asia's largest petrochemical makers with operations in Taiwan, mainland China, Indonesia and Singapore. 
				
				
					 The name "Chang Chun" in Chinese loosely means "long spring," and reflects the wish among the three that their bonds would endure.
