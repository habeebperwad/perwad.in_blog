
			Ivan Glasenberg holds the biggest private stake in the world's biggest commodity trader, Glencore, with 8.4% of the company. Glasenberg earned his MBA from the University of Southern California in 1983 and got his start with the late legendary oil trader Marc Rich, working as a marketer in the coal and coke department of Marc Rich & Co.  in his native South Africa. By the time management bought out Marc Rich & Co. and re-branded it as Glencore in 1994, Glasenberg had risen to the post of worldwide director. He burst into the billionaire club in 2011 when Glencore went public in 2011. He is a former South African race-walking champion.
