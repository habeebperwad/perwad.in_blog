
				
					De Carvalho-Heineken is one of the world's richest women thanks to her 23% controlling stake in Heineken.
				
				
					Now an executive director, she inherited the stake in 2002 from her late father, longtime CEO Freddy Heineken.
				
				
					Her husband Michael, an investment banker, and son Alexander are both on Heineken's board.
				
				
					Heineken opened a brewery in Amsterdam 150 years ago. Now the company sells 250 premium beers across the globe including Red Stripe and Lagunitas.
