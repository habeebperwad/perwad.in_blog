
				
					As vice chairman of Berkshire Hathaway, Munger is Warren Buffett's right-hand man.
				
				
					He is also chairman of publisher Daily Journal Corp. and sits on the board of retailer Costco.
				
				
					The Omaha native left the Univ. of Michigan to become an Army meteorologist during World War II.
				
				
					Munger graduated from Harvard Law School in 1948 and met Buffett at a dinner party 11 years later.
				
				
					Like Buffett, he's a pragmatic investor and a major philanthropist, giving millions to universities.
