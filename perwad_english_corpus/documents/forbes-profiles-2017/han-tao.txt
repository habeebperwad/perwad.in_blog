
				
					Han Tao chairs Shenzhen-listed Goldenmax International Technology, a supplier of  copper-clad laminates products used to make printed circuit boards.

				
				
					Han worked at a watch parts factory in Lin'an in Hangzhou from 1977-83,  studied at Zhejiang Radio and Television University in 1983-86, and then returned to the watch factory through 1989 when he worked for the predecessor of Goldenmax.
