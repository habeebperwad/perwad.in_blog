
				
					Ishihara and his immediate family own about 45% of Tokyo-listed pachinko machine maker Heiwa.
				
				
					Ishihara is not involved in running Heiwa.
				
				
					He founded another pachinko firm, Olympia, which Heiwa acquired in a stock swap in 2007.
				
				
					Heiwa subsidiary PGM is Japan's second-largest golf course operator.
