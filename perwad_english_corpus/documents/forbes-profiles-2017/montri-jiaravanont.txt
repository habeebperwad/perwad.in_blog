
			Montri Jiaravanont is the older brother of Thai billionaire Dhanin Chearavanont and honorary chairman and director of the Charoen Pokphand Group, the family's sprawling conglomerate. Montri  worked in the agroindustrial businesses and was executive director for two years at listed CP Pokphand Co.  He is listed separately from his better-known sibling based on his ownership of a 12.63% stake in the family's vast assets. His wealth is up on new information on their private holdings.
