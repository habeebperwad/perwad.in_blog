
			Civil engineer Thongma Vijitpongpun founded Pruksa in 1993 and build it intoThailand's second-largest property developer known for its low-to middle income housing. In a restructuring last year, he delisted Pruksa Real Estate and formed a new holding outfit, Pruksa Holding.  Thongma is now looking to expand his company's presence in the premium segment. He enjoys reading books on Buddhist philosophy and tending to his fruit orchards.
