
			Radik Shaimiev co-owns the TAIF Group, a large regional holding company with assets in the Republic of Tatarstan, with his brother Airat and two other billionaires - Albert Shigaboutdinov and Rustem Sulteev.   The company includes oil refineries, chemical factories, power plants and construction companies. TAIF became a shareholder in most of its companies during Russia's privatization in the mid '90s. Radik's father, Mintimer Shaimiev, was Tatarstan's first president. He also co-owns $1.5 billion (assets) Avers Bank. An avid race car driver, he won a European competition called Autocross in 2003.
