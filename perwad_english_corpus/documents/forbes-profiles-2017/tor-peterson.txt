
			Tor Peterson is the sole American among the five-person cadre of billionaires at Swiss commodities-trading powerhouse Glencore. He joined the firm in 1992. Peterson leads the coal operation for the company.  Before joining Glencore, Peterson traded commodities for six years at Phibro-Salomon in New York. He went to boarding school in Switzerland, university at Duke in the U.S. and lives in Zug. His brother, Gunnar, is a personal trainer in Los Angeles whose client list has reportedly included Sophia Vergara, Angelina Jolie, and Kim Kardashian.
