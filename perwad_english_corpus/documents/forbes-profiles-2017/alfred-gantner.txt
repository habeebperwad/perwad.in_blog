
			Alfred Gantner is co-founder of Partners Group, a Swiss private equity firm with $30 billion in assets under management. He started Partners Group in 1996 after stints at Goldman Sachs and McKinsey & Co.  Partners Group's co-founder Marcel Erni and Urs Wietlisbach are also billionaires and also worked at Goldman Sachs. Gantner owns 10% of the publicly traded company having sold about 4% of his stake since the company went public in 2006. Gantner earned his MBA from the Brigham Young University Marriott School of Management, Utah.
