
				
					Jim Walton is the youngest son of Wal-Mart founder Sam Walton.
				
				
					He runs the family's Arvest Bank, which boasts assets of more than $16 billion.
				
				
					He sat on Wal-Mart's board for more than a decade before yielding the seat to his son, Steuart, in June 2016.
				
				
					Collectively Jim and other heirs of Sam Walton own more than half of Wal-Mart's stock.
