
				
					Yang Huiyan owns 55% of real estate developer Country Garden Holdings, a stake transferred to her by her father, Yeung Kwok Keung, in 2007.
				
				
					Country Garden has partnered with an arm of the Malaysian government to develop Forest City, a $36 billion residential project near the border of Malaysia and Singapore.

				
				
					Restrictions this year on overseas real estate investment by mainland Chinese that could hurt sales there haven't done much to dent Country Garden's stock price.

				
				
					Yang's younger sister Ziyang also sits on Country Garden's board.

				
				
					 Yang holds a degree from Ohio State University.
