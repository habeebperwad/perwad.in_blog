
			From his spare bedroom in Bristol, England, Peter Hargreaves and pal Stephan Lansdown founded an eponymous financial-services firm in 1981. Both are billionaires today, and Hargreaves Lansdown controls $70 billion in assets under management with 876,00 clients.  Hargreaves, a chartered accountant for businesses like KPMG before starting his company, is the executive director.
