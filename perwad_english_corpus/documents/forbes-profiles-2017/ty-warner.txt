
			The Beanie Babies creator now gets a chunk of his fortune from his high-end-hotel portfolio, which includes the Four Seasons in New York. His Las Ventanas al Paraiso resort in Mexico recently opened the Ty Warner Mansion, which rents for $35,000 a night.  Things could have gone very differently for Warner. He was convicted of tax evasion and paid a $53 million fine in 2013 for stashing money in a Swiss bank but avoided jail time.
