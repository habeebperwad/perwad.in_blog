
				
					Zhou Hongyi is the cofounder, CEO and chairman of Beijing-based Internet security and search company Qihoo 360 Technology, which designs and develops Internet security software. 


				
				
					Zhou previously founded 3721.com, an online search company that was purchased by Yahoo.
				
				
					Qihoo delisted from the New York Stock Exchange in 2016 and aims to relist in China.
