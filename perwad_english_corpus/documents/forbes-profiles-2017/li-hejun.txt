
				
					Li chairs Hanergy Holding Group, which owns hydropower plants, a wind farm, some solar assets and a controlling stake in Hanergy Thin Film Power. 

				
				
					Trading of Hanergy Thin Film, his Hong Kong-listed solar power equipment arm, has been halted since May 2015 after a nearly 50% drop wiped out $19 billion of market capitalization.

				
				
					As part of a regulatory investigation into trading in Hanergy, Li in January agreed to be disallowed from managing a business in Hong Kong "for a certain period."
