
			High school dropout Chua Thian Poh got start making hooks and spikes for the logging industry. He went on to found luxury property developer Ho Bee, known for building high-end condos in Sentosa. The company is holding its own in a weak market due to a diversified portfolio that includes assets overseas, notably in London where it owns rent-yielding office properties.  In Singapore, Ho Bee owns the Metropolis twin-tower office complex. Chua is Singapore's non-resident high commissioner to the Republic of the Maldives.
