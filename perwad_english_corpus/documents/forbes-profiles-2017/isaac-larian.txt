
				
					Larian's fortune comes from toymaker MGA Entertainment, maker of Little Tikes, Bratz dolls and Lalaloopsy dolls.
				
				
					Larian left Iran in 1971 on a one-way ticket, then put himself through Cal State, Los Angeles, as a waiter.
				
				
					With his brother, he started an import-export business, shipping brass lawn ornaments.
				
				
					MGA started importing electronics like Nintendo's early handheld videogame systems before eventually designing toys.
