
			Harindarpal Banga, former vice chairman of the Noble Group, made his money over a 21-year career with Asia's largest commodity trader before cashing in the last of his shares in 2012. He started Hong Kong-based Caravel Group with sons Angad and Guneet in 2013  to trade and ship commodities.  Originally from Punjab, India, he's lived in Hong Kong since the 1970s. Harry was honored in 2011 with the Pravasi Bharatiya Samman Award, the highest distinction conferred on overseas Indians.
