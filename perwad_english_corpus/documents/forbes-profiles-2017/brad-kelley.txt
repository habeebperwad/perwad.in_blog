
			With 1.5 million acres from Hawaii to Florida, Brad Kelley is one of the largest landowners in the U.S. The Kentucky native and farmer's son built a fortune from discount cigarettes. He built Commonwealth Brands, maker of USA Gold and Malibu cigarettes, and sold it for $1 billion in 2001. He bought travel guide Lonely Planet from the BBC for $75 million in 2013. His horse English Channel has sired offspring including 154 winners that have nabbed nearly $20 million in prize money.
