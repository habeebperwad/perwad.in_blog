
			Binod Chaudhary controls the Cinnovation/CG Group and is Nepal's sole billionaire. His biggest asset is a controlling stake in Nepal's Nabil Bank, but CG's portfolio also includes foods, electronics, beer and hotels.  His CG Foods, maker of the popular Wai Wai noodles, has overseas factories in India, Serbia, Saudia Arabia and Bangladesh. CG's hotel assets consist of 29 owned and 70 managed properties, including a string of luxury hotels with India's Taj hotel chain. His late father opened the country's first department store. Chaudhary skipped college to join the family trade at age 18. His three sons work with him. The English edition of his autobiography, Making it Big, was published last year.
