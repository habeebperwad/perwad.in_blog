
			Chemical engineer from MIT, Kishore Mariwala gets his wealth from his stake in Indian consumer goods outfit Marico, which his billionaire nephew Harsh Mariwala oversees. He used to sit on the company's board and still has a board seat in Marico's non-profit arm, Marico Innovation Foundation.   An avid sailor, he is also a founder-board member of the Indian Institute of Human Settlements. Along with wife Shalini, he backs several non-profits.
