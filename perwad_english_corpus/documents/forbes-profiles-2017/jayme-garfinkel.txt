
			Jayme Brasil Garfinkel is chairman and the biggest shareholder of insurance giant Porto Seguro. Garfinkel's family bought Porto Seguro in 1972 and he took a job at the company as a director's assistant. Six years later, after his father's death, he became the firm's vice president.  He took the company public in November 2004 and was its CEO from 2006 to 2012. Porto Seguro boasts more than 15,000 employees. Itau Unibanco, Latin America's largest bank owns about 30% of Porto Seguro.  Garfinkel has supported the creation and maintenance of schools for young children in favelas, or shantytowns, around Sao Paulo, supporting over 50,000 students.
