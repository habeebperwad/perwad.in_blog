
				
					Luo founded Cheng Shin Rubber in 1967 as a tire supplier to Taiwan's then expanding bicycle industry.

				
				
					Today the company, known as CST, is run by his son Lo Tsai-Jen.
				
				
					It is one of the world's largest makers of tires and tubes for motorcycles, all-terrain vehicles, cars, bicycles, forklifts and lawn and garden equipment.
