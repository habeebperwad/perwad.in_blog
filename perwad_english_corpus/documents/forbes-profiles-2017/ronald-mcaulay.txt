
			Ronald McAulay owns a big stake in CLP Holdings, the power company chaired by brother-in-law Michael Kadoorie, and in The Hongkong & Shanghai Hotels, which operates the famous Peninsula Hotel in Hong Kong.  He is a member of the international council of the Tate Gallery and a trustee of the Victoria & Albert Museum, both in London. His son, Andrew, is chairman of Kadoorie Farm & Botanic Garden in the New Territories.
