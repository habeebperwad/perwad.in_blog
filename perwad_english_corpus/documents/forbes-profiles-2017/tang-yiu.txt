
			Tang Yiu founded footwear and sportswear retailer Belle International in 1982. Top brands include Teenmix and Staccato. The company went public on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange in 2007. Its share price was down more than a third in 2016 on sluggish consumer spending in China.  Tang chairs the Federation of Hong Kong Footwear and is the honorary president of the Sam Shui Natives Association. Son Tang King Loy is a director and senior vice president.
