
			Judith Neilson is the ex-wife of billionaire Kerr Neilson and is now a billionaire herself. She now gets credit for her stake in international equities manager Platinum Asset Management, founded in 1994 by her former husband.  She's also reported to have her own property portfolio. The Zimbabwean-born former graphic designer is founder and director of Sydney's White Rabbit Gallery, which showcases  one of the world's biggest collections of contemporary Chinese art post-2000. She's building another Sydney art gallery and performance space called Phoenix.
