
			Indonesian billionaire Eka Tjipta Widjaja's son, Oei Hong Leong, is a Singapore citizen and long-time resident of the island-state.  Oei, who earlier sued Goldman Sachs for selling him lossmaking currency options, is embroiled in a legal dispute against Canada's Concord Pacific Acquisitions over a proposed waterfront development in downtown Vancouver.  Oei is an avid collector of Buddhist art and owns a private museum in Singapore with 50,000 items.
