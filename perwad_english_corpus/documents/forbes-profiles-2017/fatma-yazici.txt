
			Fatma Tuba Yazici and her three sons became the largest shareholders in Diler Holding when her husband, Recep Sami Yazici, died suddenly in 2009. Its assets include Diler Iron and Steel, Yazici Iron and Steel, Diler Investment Bank and the 2,000-bed Cornelia Deluxe Hotel in Antalya.  The holding also owns a port that exports to 50 countries and Atlas Energy, whose assets include thermal and hydroelectric power plants. When it comes to publicity, Fatma Yazici is among the most reticent of Turkish billionaires. She doesn't grant interviews and or provide photographs to the media, for fear of being recognized on the street.
