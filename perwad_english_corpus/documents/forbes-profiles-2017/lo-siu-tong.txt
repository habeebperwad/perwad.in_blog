
			Lo Siu-tong started redeveloping old buildings in 1966 and today his Yu Tai Hing Co. owns an array of offices, residences and shops that includes the prime 100 Queen's Road Central office building. He also owns a chain of 10 budget hotels under the Bridal Tea House brand.  Youngest son, Dennis Law, runs the company and is also a movie scriptwriter, producer and director.
