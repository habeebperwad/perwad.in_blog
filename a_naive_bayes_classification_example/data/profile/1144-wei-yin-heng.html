Wei and his 3 brothers --Ing-Chou, Yin-Chun and Ying-Chiao--founded Cayman Islands-based Tingyi. Tingyi is now a big producer of ready-to-drink tea and bottled water in China; it also supplies half of the country's instant noodle market. The family also has investments in Taipei 101, Taiwan's tallest building, and Dicos fried chicken chain.
