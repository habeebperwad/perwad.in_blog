Brin serves as president of Google parent company Alphabet. He previously ran the secretive Google X division, which made the ill-fated Google glasses. Brin cofounded Google with Larry Page in 1998 after they met at Stanford University. The richest immigrant in America, Brin has been an outspoken critic of Trump's immigration ban. Brin came to the U.S. from Russia when he was 6 in the wake of anti-Semitism against his family.
