Fredrik Lundberg is CEO of L.E. Lundbergforetagen AB, founded by his father Lars in 1944 as a construction outfit. Fredrik took over management of the firm in 1981. Today it's a diversified holding company with interests in property management and other investments. Daughters Louise and Katarina, also billionaires, both sit on the board and each owns a 14% stake in the group. Lundberg moved his family to Switzerland in 1985 to avoid punitive taxes but later returned to Sweden. He enjoys hunting and was once a junior curling world champion.
