Long-time China advertising industry entrepreneur Jason Jiang leads Focus Media, the Chinese outdoor advertising firm that delisted in the U.S. in 2013 following negative publicity from a short seller. Focus Media was acquired by Hedy Holdings, a listed Chinese computer manufacturer, in a backdoor listing at home in China in 2016. Focus has largely stayed ahead of the trends in out-of-home advertising in China over the years, most recently profiting from movie theatre shorts a time of box office growth.
