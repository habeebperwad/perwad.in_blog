Abhay Firodia is chairman of Force Motors, maker of vans, pickup trucks, SUVs and tractors, which has been run by his son Prasan since 2009. Formerly known as Bajaj Tempo when it was a joint venture with the Bajaj clan, it was founded by his late father, Navalmal Firodia, in 1958. The families fell out in 1968 after a bitter feud. He continues to hold valuable stakes in companies of the Bajaj family. His privately held Jaya Hind Industries, which has a joint venture with French auto parts maker Montupet, supplies cylinder heads to Ford from a new factory in Madhya Pradesh state.
