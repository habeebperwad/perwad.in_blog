Kenneth Lo founded Crystal Group with his wife in 1970 and built a modest sweater factory into one of Asia's largest garment manufacturers, with 48,000 employees at 19 sites around the world. Notable customers include Levi's, Victoria's Secret, Abercrombie & Fitch and J. C. Penney. Lo is chairman of the group while his son Andrew is CEO. He is the eldest son of late textile magnate Law Ting-pong. His brother is fellow billionaire Law Kar Po.
