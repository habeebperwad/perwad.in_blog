Known as the "King of Diamonds," Lev Leviev owns an integrated diamond outfit with mines in places like South Africa and Botswana and a high end jewelry boutique in New York City. He made his name undercutting the DeBeers diamond cartel, striking his own deals with diamond-producing countries like Russia and Angola. Leviev is also the controlling shareholder of Africa Israel Investments, a real estate and construction company listed on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange. Leviev was born in Uzbekistan. His Lubavitcher Jewish family fled to Israel in 1971.
