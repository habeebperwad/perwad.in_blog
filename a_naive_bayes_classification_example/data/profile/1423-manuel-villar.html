Manuel Villar is majority owner of Starmalls, one of the Philippines' largest mall operators, and Vista Land & Lifescapes, a property developer. In July of 2016 the firm announced plans to invest $25 million to open 7 more retail stores within the next year that will sell home furnishings through its AllHome brand. The family-owned Golden Haven Memorial Park, a property developer affiliate of Vista Land focused on parks, went public in June of 2016. Villar's son Paolo runs Vista. As a child, Manuel helped his mom sell seafood in a Manila market.
