Helen Johnson-Leipold is the CEO of Johnson Outdoors, a publicly-traded company that sells outdoor gear such as tents and kayaks. She is also the chairman of the Johnson Financial Group, which includes family-owned Johnson Bank. Along with her three siblings and mother, she's an heir to the SC Johnson fortune. The family-owned company was founded in 1886 by Helen's great-great grandfather, SC Johnson. Today her brother Fisk leads the cleaning products giant, which owns such well-known brands as Ziploc, Glade, Drano and Pledge. Helen and her family live in Racine, Wisc.
