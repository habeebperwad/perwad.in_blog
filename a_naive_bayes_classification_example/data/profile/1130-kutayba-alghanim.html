Alghanim chairs diversified firm Alghanim Industries, which his late father Yusuf founded in the 1930s. It has operations in 40 countries. Alghanim Industries sells GM, Ford and Honda cars in Kuwait, owns a consumer electronics retailer, manufactures insulation and steel products, and distributes food; Kutayba's son Omar is the CEO. Kutayba was knighted by Queen Elizabeth II in 2015. Since 2009, Kutayba and his brother Bassam have fought over division of the assets they inherited. A U.S. judge put a case on hold, pending arbitration in Kuwait.
