Fifth generation scion of storied Burman clan, Anand Burman, has been chairman of family's Dabur, India's fourth largest consumer goods company, for a decade. His wealth is derived from a 17% stake in the Delhi-headquartered company, which is known for its hair oil, fruit juices and health supplements. Aside from the family business, Burman has invested in his personal capacity in home healthcare services, cancer diagnostics and life sciences. He enjoys playing the saxophone.
