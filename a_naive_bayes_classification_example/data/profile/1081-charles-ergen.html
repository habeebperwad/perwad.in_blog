He's the son of an Austrian nuclear physicist who worked at the Oak Ridge National Laboratory in Oak Ridge, Tenn. After college he was a professional gambler, but apparently got thrown out of a Vegas casino for counting cards. In 1980 he began selling satellite dishes in rural Denver from the back of a truck, which became DISH Network. Four years after leaving DISH chairman role Ergen returned as CEO in 2015.
