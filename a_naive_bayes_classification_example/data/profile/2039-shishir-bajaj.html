Shishir Bajaj, a member of the Bajaj clan, who split from his older brother Rahul and cousins in 2008, returns to the Forbes Billionaires list on the back of new information about his energy assets. His privately held companies, Lalitpur Power and Bajaj Energy, have three power units in the state of Uttar Pradesh, generating a combined 2,430 megawatts. His portfolio also consists of a controlling stake in consumer goods flagship Bajaj Corp, which sells hair oil and skin care products, and sugar unit Bajaj Hindusthan, which is benefiting from a recovery in sugar prices. Bajaj's son Kushagra runs the group as vice chairman.
