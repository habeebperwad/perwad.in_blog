Kwek Leng Beng is the executive chairman of Singapore's Hong Leong Group, founded by his father and uncles in 1941. He's also executive chairman of City Developments, the island state's second-largest property developer. Amid constraints at home, Kwek has pressed on with an overseas expansion, acquiring assets and launching new projects in the UK, China and Australia in recent years. At home, the hotel mogul opened M Social, a 293-room boutique hotel designed by Philippe Starck, in June. He's also partnered with American hotelier Ian Schrager for Singapore's first Edition Hotel, due to open in 2019.
