After a 20-year absence, Prajogo Pangestu returns to the billionaire ranks. His fortune increased more than fivefold in the 12 months through mid-February 2017 as the value of his stakes in petrochemicals company Barito Pacific and its listed subsidiary Chandra Asri Petrochemical both jumped. In November 2016 Chandra Asri announced plans to team with Houston polyethylene-technology licensor Univation Technologies to develop a new plant in Banten, west of Jakarta.
