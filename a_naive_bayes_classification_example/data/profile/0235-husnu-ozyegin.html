Husnu Ozyegin's Credit Europe Bank has assets of over $9 billion and more than three million customers in nine European and two Asian countries. In Turkey he operates under the brand name Fibabanka. Financial services, which also include leasing, factoring and insurance, are grouped under Fina Holding. Under sister Fiba Holding are investments in retail, real estate, health, renewable energy, ports and hotels. In 2014 Ozyegin made his first investment in the health sector, becoming a 50% partner in the Florence Nightingale Hospital. In 2016 he bought the Kadikoy Sifa Hospital in Istanbul.
