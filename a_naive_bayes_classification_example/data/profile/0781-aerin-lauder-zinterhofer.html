Aerin Zinterhofer Lauder's launched her own lifestyle brand within her grandmother Estee Lauder's cosmetics giant. Aerin's brand started out with makeup and then a floral perfume line, and has since expanded into accessories, jewelry and decor. Lauder meanwhile still works at the publicly traded cosmetics giant founded by her grandmother as its image and style director. She and her sister Jane (also a billionaire) have spent almost all of their professional lives working at the family company; Aerin started during summer breaks at UPenn. Her husband (and college sweetheart) Eric Zinterhofer is a Harvard M.B.A. who co-founded private equity firm Searchlight Capital in 2010 after 12 years at billionaire Leon Black's Apollo Global Management.
