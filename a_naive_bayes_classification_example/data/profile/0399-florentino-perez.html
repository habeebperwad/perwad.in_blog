Florentino Perez splits his time between Spain's largest construction company, Grupo ACS (Actividades de Construccion y Servicios) and the world's most valuable sports team, Real Madrid. Perez essentially invented Real Madrid's modern business model, signing soccer stars like David Beckham and Zinedine Zidane in his first tenure as president, between 2000 and 2006, boosting commercial revenues and making the team number one in terms of worldwide sales. Florentino was re-elected in 2009, and led Real Madrid to become the world's most valuable soccer club in 2013. Florentino has also been president and CEO of Grupo ACS since 1997. He owns a 12.6% stake in the publicly-traded construction company.
