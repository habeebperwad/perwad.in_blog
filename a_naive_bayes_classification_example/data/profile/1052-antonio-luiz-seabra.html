Founder of Natura Cosmeticos, Brazil's largest beauty company, Antonio Luiz Seabra created the company as Brazil's answer to Avon in 1969. It sells products door-to-door across Brazil in addition to other Latin American countries and France. Together with Natura partner Guilherme Peirao Leal, who ran as vice president in Brazil's 2010 election campaign, Seabra also owns Bresco, a Brazilian property-rental operation focused on industrial property, warehouses, offices and hotels. Seabra communicates directly to Natura's employees through motivational videos. He holds a bachelor's in economics and is known for quoting poetry and philosophy in business meetings. He also owns real estate in London and Paris.
