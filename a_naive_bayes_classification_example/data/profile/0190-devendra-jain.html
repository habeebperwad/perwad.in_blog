Devendra Jain started working in his family's paper and newsprint trading business at age 18. He now presides over the $ 2 billion (revenue) Inox Group with interests in industrial gases, fluorocarbons, cinema multiplexes and cryogenic engineering. Listed wind energy arm Inox Wind, run by grandson Devansh, has a capacity of 1,600 megawatts. The group is now better known for its nationwide chain of 116 multiplexes.
