After working as a stockbroker and finance executive, Costantini founded real estate development firm Consultatio S.A. in 1991. The Buenos Aires-traded firm has developments in Argentina and the United States, including a luxury residential complex near Miami that cost $700 million to build. He's also a majority shareholder of Consultatio Asset Management, a Buenos Aires-based firm with about $720 million of assets under of management. It mostly invests in Latin America.
