Michael and Marian Ilitch cofounded Little Caesar's Pizza, which sells $4.2 billion worth of pizzas worldwide. Michael, who also owned the Detroit Tigers baseball team, passed away in early February 2017 at age 87. He is survived by his wife Marian and 7 children. In 2016 the Ilitches started construction on a new hockey arena for their Detroit Red Wings hockey team, anchoring a new $1.2 billion entertainment district in Detroit, financed through a public-private partnership. Marian owns the Motor City Casino, which had a solid 2016. Before he died, Michael and Marian Ilitch donated $40 million plus land for a new Mike Ilitch School of Business at Wayne State University.
