Sankai is president of robot maker Cyberdyne; he founded the firm in 2004. Its key product is the Robot Suit HAL (Hybrid Assistive Limb), whose robot arms and legs take over when our own fail as a result of age or physical impairment. Its HAL suits read electrical pulses in patients' nerves and offer the potential to restore muscle movement. The company rents its HAL suits to hospitals and nursing homes. In early 2016 the Japanese government approved national health insurance coverage for medical use of the suits.
