Lukas Walton is the grandson of Wal-Mart founder Sam Walton. He inherited his fortune when his father, John Walton, died in a 2005 plane crash. He received about one-third of his father's estate; his mother, Christy Walton, got about one-sixth. He owns stakes in Wal-Mart, First Solar and Arvest Bank, though he does not work for the companies. Little is known about Walton, who is said to be an active investor and philanthropist.
