Anttonen heads $6 billion (sales) Scandinavian fuel distributor St1 Nordic Oy and oil refinery St1 Group Oy. He uses profits to fund subsidiaries combating climate change like wind power, geothermal heat and waste-based ethanol fuel. He got his start as an oil trader working for Finland-based oil refiner and marketer, Neste Oyj, before founding his own in 1995.
