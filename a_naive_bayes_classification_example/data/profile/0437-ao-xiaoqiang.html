Ao Xiaoqiang chairs Beijing SDL Technology, a supplier of environmental monitoring equipment to industries such as power, steel, chemicals and waste incineration. Beijing SDL's business partners include Korbi of South Korea, with which it has a water-monitoring equipment joint venture. Ao graduated from South China University of Technology with a major in automation, and worked at Beijing Beifen-Ruili Analytical Instrument (Group) as senior engineer before he quit in 1998 and took the plunge as an entrepreneur. SDL listed at the Shenzhen Stock Exchange in March 2012.
