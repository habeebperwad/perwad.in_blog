Infosys cofounder Nandan Nilekani, who is credited with building Aadhar, India's mammoth identity card scheme, while chairman of the Unique Identification Authority of India, is now backing startups. He is the biggest investor in lunar firm TeamIndus, which is building a spacecraft to land on the moon. He teamed up with Ratan Tata, former chairman of the Tata Group, to start Avanti Finance, which will provide microloans to the poor. He's also invested in auto parts maker Sedemac Mechatronics, whose founders studied at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, his alma mater.
