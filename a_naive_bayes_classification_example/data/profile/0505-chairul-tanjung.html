CT Corp. chairman Chairul Tanjung is bullish about his retail business after securing a nearly $400 million investment from Singapore's sovereign wealth fund, GIC, in February 2016. His Trans Retail already operates stores under the Carrefour and Transmart brands. His group also controls the Wendy's franchise in Indonesia and has the Versace, Mango and Jimmy Choo franchises. Tanjung's Bank Mega is one of Indonesia's largest issuers of credit cards, with more than 1.2 million issued. The journalist's son sold study guides while attending school to become a dentist.
