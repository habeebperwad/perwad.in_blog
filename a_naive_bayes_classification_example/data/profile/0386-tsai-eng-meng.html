Tsai chairs Want Want China, one of the world's largest suppliers of Chinese-style snacks and drinks. Tsai built up Want Want from his father's small trading outfit, I Lan Foods Industrial, in Taiwan. He also invests in hotels, financial services and media including China Times newspaper and China Television Co. Sons Kevin and Wang-Chia along with nephew Cheng Wen-Hsien are members of Want Want's board of directors.
