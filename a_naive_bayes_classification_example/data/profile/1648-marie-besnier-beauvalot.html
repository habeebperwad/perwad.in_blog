Marie Besnier Beauvalot, along with bothers Emmanuel and Jean Michel, inherited French dairy giant Lactalis, based in their ancestral hometown of Laval in western France. Her father founded the company, which sells over $18 billion of products including President brie, Milkmaid yogurts and Valbreso Feta. Emmanuel runs the company. The biggest dairy producer in Europe, Lactalis continues to expand internationally in places like China and Southeast Asia. The family's holding companies also own stakes in Italy's publicly traded Parmalat and Frommageries Bel, the French maker of Baby Bel, La Vache Qui Rit and Boursin.
