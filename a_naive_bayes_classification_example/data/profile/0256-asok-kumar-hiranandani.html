Co-founder of erstwhile Royal Brothers property group, Asok Kumar Hiranandani, separated from his older brother Raj Kumar in 2011 after dividing their property empire. Today, his Royal Group Holdings, run by son Bobby, owns such trophies as the 215-room Sofitel Singapore Sentosa Resort and the So Softel Hotel in Singapore's central business district. Royal's overseas portfolio includes assets in Australia, Malaysia and Indonesia. Son Bobby's wedding celebration, held at their Sentosa resort in December, drew more than 3,000 guests.
