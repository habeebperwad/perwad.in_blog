The Pritzker Group, which Anthony "Tony" Pritzker runs along with his brother Jay Robert ("J.B.") Pritzker, is a private equity and venture capital firm whose investments include a stake in Elon Musk's SpaceX. Tony is an heir to the Chicago-based Pritzker family fortune. He owns slightly more than 4% of Hyatt, which is chaired by his cousin Thomas Pritzker. Tony spent 20 years running middle-market manufacturing and distribution companies, including firms within the Pritzker family's Marmon conglomerate. He is a trustee of the Los Angeles County Museum of Art, and one of 11 billionaire Pritzker family members.
