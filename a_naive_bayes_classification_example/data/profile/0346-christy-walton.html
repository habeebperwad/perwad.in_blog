Christy Walton married into what became the richest family in the world: Wal-Mart's Waltons. She inherited her fortune when her husband, John Walton, died in a 2005 plane crash. She received about one-sixth of his fortune, mostly stakes in Wal-Mart, First Solar and Arvest Bank. Her son, Lukas Walton, inherited roughly one-third of John's estate and is also a billionaire. She leads a very private life in Jackson, Wyoming.
