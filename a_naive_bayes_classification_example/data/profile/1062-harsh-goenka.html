Harsh Goenka, fifth generation of a storied Indian business clan, chairs the $3.1 billion (revenue) RPG Group, named after his late father who divided the family empire between him and younger sibling Sanjiv. Key companies in the diversified conglomerate, are tire maker Ceat, run by son Anant, and software outfit Zensar Technologies. Keen to back startups, Goenka has set up RPG Ventures and encourages staff to become entrepreneurs. One new venture is an online store for senior citizens. Goenka is an avid art collector.
