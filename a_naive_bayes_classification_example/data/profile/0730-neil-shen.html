Neil Shen is the founding managing partner of Sequoia China, where he has been ranked as one of the country's top venture capital investors for much of the past decade. A co-founder of travel site Ctrip, Shen had previously worked at Deutsche Bank Hong Kong, Chemical Bank, Lehman Brothers and Citibank in various investment banking areas. Shen holds a master's degree from in management from Yale University and an undergraduate degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
