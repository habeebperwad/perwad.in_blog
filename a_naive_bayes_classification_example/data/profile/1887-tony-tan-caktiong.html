Tony Tan Caktiong's Jollibee empire continues to expand across the world and attract legions of loyal customers. Among the new hot spots of 2016: Skokie, Illinois, where hundreds of customers lined up for an opening in August. Jollibee is the fastest-growing Asian restaurant chain in the world, selling Filipino, Chinese, American and European dishes adapted to a modern, quick-serve, affordable format. With partner Edgar Sia II, he also owns stake in the fast-growing DoubleDragon Properties Corp.
