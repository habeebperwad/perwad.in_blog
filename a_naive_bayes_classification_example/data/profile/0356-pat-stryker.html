Pat Stryker's grandfather Homer Stryker (d: 1980) was an orthopedic surgeon who invented and patented medical devices and later founded Stryker Corporation, which had $11.3 billion in sales in 2016. Born in Michigan, she now lives in Fort Collins, Colo. , and is the owner of Bohemian Companies, a group that includes venture capital activities and real estate development. Her private Bohemian Foundation supports youth and community programs. In December 2016, she and her brother, Jon, gave $5 million each to fund the nation's memorial to the victims of racial terror lynchings. Their sister Ronda is also on the list.
