Tse Ping is a member of the billionaire Dhanin clan from Thailand and guiding hand behind Hong Kong-listed pharmaceutical maker Sino Biopharmaceutical. He stepped down as chairman of Sino Biopharmaceutical in 2015 and ceded the role to his 22-year-old daughter Theresa, who has an undergraduate degree in economics from the Wharton School, while staying on as an executive director.
