Abdul Majeed Alhokair started the Fawaz Abdulaziz Alhokair Group with his two brothers in 1989 with just two menswear stores. Today it is one of Saudi Arabia's most valuable retail and real estate companies with 19 shopping malls across the kingdom. The brothers' publicly-traded company, Fawaz Abdulaziz Alhokair Co., holds the franchise rights to global apparel brands including Zara, Banana Republic, Gap, Nine West and Topshop.
