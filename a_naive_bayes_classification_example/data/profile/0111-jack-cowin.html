Canadian-born fast-food mogul Jack Cowin has built his fortune on burgers, fried chicken and pizza. He's the founder and executive chairman of Sydney-based Competitive Foods Australia, which operates Burger King's Australian franchise as Hungry Jack's. It boasts 400 stores and a food-processing division. He's also the top shareholder of Domino's Pizza Enterprises, the listed Australian franchisee of the U.S. parent company. Cowin started with one KFC outlet in Perth, after emigrating in 1969. He was appointed chancellor of alma mater Western University in Ontario, Canada in 2015.
