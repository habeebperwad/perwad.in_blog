Sameer Gehlaut's Indiabulls Housing Finance is India's second biggest mortgage lender. It also owns a 40% stake in U.K.-based OakNorth Bank, a lender to small businesses. An engineer from India's elite Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi, he started online brokerage Indiabulls with two college pals in 1999, getting steel tycoon Lakshmi Mittal and hedge fund Farallon Capital as early investors. Took the company public in 2004 and moved into real estate scooping up prized land in Mumbai in public auctions. Gehlaut is building a luxury hotel in London's Mayfair area. His companies were inspected by the Indian tax authorities last July. The group said it follows "ethical and legal standards of the highest order" and that the inspection was routine. He owns a Global 5000 airplane.
