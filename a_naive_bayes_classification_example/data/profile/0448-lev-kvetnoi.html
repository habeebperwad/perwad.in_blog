Lev Kvetnoi has invested in airports, airport services, banking and cement.For a time he served as chief executive of a company in billionaire Alisher Usmanov's metals group, but he sold out his interests to Usmanov in 2006. The next year he bought both National Standard Bank and NovorosCement, Russia's third-largest cement producer. In 2011 he expanded his cement holdings by acquiring one of billionaire Elena Baturina's factories in southern Russia. He was the largest shareholder in Moscow's Vnukovo Airport, owning 30% of the project, until he sold out in 2013 and quit its board.
