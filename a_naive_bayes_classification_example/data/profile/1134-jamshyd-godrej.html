Jamshyd Godrej, the cousin of Godrej clan patriarch, Adi Godrej, runs privately-held consumer durables arm Godrej & Boyce as chairman and managing director along with his son Navroze, whom he is grooming as his successor. The $1.4 billion (revenues) firm is best known for its appliances, furniture and security solutions. A U.S.-trained mechanical engineer, Jamshyd is a keen sailor. His sister Smita Crishna-Godrej is also a billionaire.
