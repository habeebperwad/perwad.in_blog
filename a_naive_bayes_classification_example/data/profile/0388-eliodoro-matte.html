Eliodoro Matte is the president and, with his siblings Bernardo and Patricia (each of whom is a billionaire), the largest shareholder of CMPC, a Chilean forestry and paper products company they inherited from their late father, Eliodoro Matte Ossa. Until May 2011, Eliodoro was president of five of Grupo CMPC's holdings; despite having stepped down, he maintains leadership over Empresas CMPC, the primary holding company. The siblings also have stakes in telecom, banking and shipping firms. Eliodoro served as president of conservative Chilean think tank Public Studies Center until 2015.
