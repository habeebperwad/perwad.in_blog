Enrique Razon chairs the International Container Terminal Services (ICTSI), the country's largest port operator, which also operates through subsidiaries in eastern Europe, Africa and the Americas. In recent years it's suffered from a slowdown in global trading; income was down by almost 65% in 2015. ICTSI said it plans to revive a container rail service through the Philippines' capital in Manila. Razon's grandfather began with a port in Manila in 1916. Razon's dad rebuilt the business after World War II. Razon started out in the port business with his dad in Manila and transformed it into a global giant. His hospitality firm, Bloomberry Resorts Co., includes its crown jewel, the Solaire Casino and Resort in the Philippines.
