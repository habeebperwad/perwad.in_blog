Alexander Frolov is chief executive of multinational steel company Evraz. His partner and fellow billionaire, Alexander Abramov, is chairman. While he's still a big shareholder, Frolov unloaded some shares in 2015 when the company did a buyback. He and Abramov sold a 40% stake in Evraz for $3 billion in 2006. Today he also has a stake in Highlands Gold Mining. He got his start working as a research fellow at the I.V. Kurchatov Institute of Atomic Energy, and he has a doctorate in physics and mathematics. He holds more than $1 billion of his assets in cash. In 2016, he and Abramov bought a 5% stake in property developer, Samolet Development.
