Low-profile Rajan Raheja who hails from the sprawling Raheja property clan, has built a separate diversified empire that includes interests in cement, batteries and cable television. He returns to the billionaire ranks after shares of his battery maker Exide Industries regained momentum. The company has ramped up capacity with a new $100 million plant in eastern India. Cable television unit Hathway Cable & Datacom is planning an IPO of its subsidiary GTPL Hathway, a cable TV and broadbandservices provider.
