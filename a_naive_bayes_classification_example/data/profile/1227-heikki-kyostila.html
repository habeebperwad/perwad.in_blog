Heikki Kyöstilä is President of Planmeca Oy, one of the world's largest dental equipment manufacturers, headquartered in Helsinki, Finland. Kyostila founded Planmeca Oy in 1971 in Helsinki as a small dental stools and instrument cabinets manufacturing company. Since then, the company has expanded its product range, which now includes dental care units, 2D and 3D imaging X-ray devices, CAD/CAM products and software applications.
