A native of New Zealand, Richard Chandler relocated to Singapore in 2006 where he now runs Clermont Group, his personal investment fund. His extensive portfolio includes stakes in energy companies and one of Vietnam's largest private healthcare providers, Hoan My Medical. Chandler cofounded oil and gas exploration company Orion Energy with Altima Partners, which plans to focus on Latin America; he owns a controlling stake. He previously cofounded emerging market investment firm Sovereign Global with his brother, Christopher, after the sale of their parents' retailing business in Hong Kong. Christopher now runs Dubai-based investment firm Legatum Group.
