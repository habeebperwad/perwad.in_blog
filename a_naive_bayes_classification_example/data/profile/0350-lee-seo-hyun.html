The second daughter of Samsung Chairman Lee Kun-Hee, Lee Seo-Hyun took charge of Samsung C&T's struggling fashion division in December 2015. She is tasked by Samsung with quintupling revenue for the business by 2020. She joined Samsung Group's Cheil Industries as a manager in 2002, after graduating from the Parsons School of Design in New York. Her elder brother Jay Y. Lee, heir to the Samsung empire, was indicted and jailed on bribery charges in February 2017 and faces a landmark trial. He denies any wrongdoing.
