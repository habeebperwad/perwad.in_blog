William C. Ford's widow inherited the NFL's Detroit Lions in 2014, and instead of being a figurehead, the feisty, 91-year-old granddaughter of Harvey Firestone turned the organization upside down, much to the delight of long-suffering Lions fans. She swept out top executives and let her impatience be known. Meanwhile, Ford Motor Co. is riding high on pickup truck sales while investing heavily in self-driving, electric cars for the future.
