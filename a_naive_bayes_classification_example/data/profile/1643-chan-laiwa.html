Chan's Fu Wah International Group is one of the largest real estate developers headquartered in Beijing. Fu Wah is best known for its Jinbo Street development in the city, with Jinbao Tower, the Regent Building and the Beijing Hong Kong Jockey Club, among other structures. Fu Wah has also been expanding outside of China in recent years, investing in hotels in Australia and New Zealand. Chan is curator of the China Red Sandalwood Museum.
