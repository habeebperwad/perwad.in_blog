Physician Azad Moopen is preparing to list his Dubai-based hospital chain Aster DM Healthcare, which operates a network of 18 hospitals, 90 clinics and 200 pharmacies in the Middle East and India. Aster treats 10 million patients annually and employs more than 2,000 doctors. Last year, Aster acquired a 25% stake in Hyderabad's Ramesh Hospitals.
