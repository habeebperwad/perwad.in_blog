Galperin is founder and CEO of e-commerce platform MercadoLibre, the eBay of Latin America. MercadoLibre operates in 18 Latin American countries and Portugal. Galperin majored in economics and finance at the University of Pennsylvania and got an MBA from Stanford University. He worked at Argentinian oil company YPF for three years and as a summer intern at JPMorgan before starting MercadoLibre.
