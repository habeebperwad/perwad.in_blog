Qi Jinxing is the chairman of Hangzhou Binjiang Real Estate Group, a real estate developer headquartered in what many consider to be one of China's most scenic cities, Hangzhou. Qi is also an investor in cancer-treatment firm Ascletis. Qi got his first job in a government district office before moving on to a management position at a construction firm. In 1996 turned down a bigger government position to set up Hangzhou Binjiang.
