Ermirio Pereira de Moraes is the son of Jose Ermirio de Moraes, who founded family-owned Grupo Votorantim, one of Latin America's largest diversified industrial conglomerates. Votorantim is present in more than 20 countries through the aluminum, pulp and paper, energy, agribusiness and cement industries. The group dates back to 1918, when de Moraes' father bought a textile factory in Sao Paulo. Today the family still controls 100% of the business.
