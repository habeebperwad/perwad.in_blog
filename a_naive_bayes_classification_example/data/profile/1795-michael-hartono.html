Michael Hartono and his brother Budi Hartono, whose fortune is listed separately, are the richest people in Indonesia. Michael's most valuable holding is a stake in publicly-traded Bank Central Asia, one of the biggest financial institutions in the country. Another part of his fortune is in kretek, a type of cigarette that's a blend of cloves, tobacco and other ingredients. The Hartono's company Djarum is the one of the country's largest kretek manufacturers.
