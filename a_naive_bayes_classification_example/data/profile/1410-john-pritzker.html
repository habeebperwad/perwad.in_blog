John Pritzker learned the hotel business at his family's Hyatt Hotel chain and has used that knowledge to build Commune Hotels and Resorts, his hotel management company. In January 2016 Commune Hotels announced a merger with Destination Hotels. The combined company manages some 90 properties in 7 countries. Pritzker sold his shares in Hyatt to relatives in 2010. His private equity firm, Geolo Capital, acquired a majority stake (for an undisclosed price) in Alila Hotels & Resorts of Singapore in 2015. In August 2015, Pritzker inked a deal with Chinese autoparts maker Wanxiang Group, which will invest $1 billion with Pritzker in U.S. hotels. Its first purchase was the Ventana Inn & Spa in Big Sur, California. Pritzker a member of the extended Pritzker family of Chicago; his brother Tom is chairman of Hyatt Hotels and his sister Gigi is a movie producer.
