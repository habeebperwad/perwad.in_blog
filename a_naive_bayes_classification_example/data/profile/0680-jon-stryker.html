Jon Stryker is the grandson of Homer Stryker (d. 1980), an orthopedic surgeon who founded Kalamazoo, Mich.-based Stryker Corp., which sold $11.3 billion in medical equipment and software in 2016. His Arcus Foundation supports LGBT issues and protection for apes, two favorite causes. He funded the new Arcus Center for Social Justice Leadership at Kalamazoo College, and in 2015 donated land and money to expand the Kalamazoo Nature Center. In December 2016, he and his sister Pat gave $5 million each to fund the nation's memorial to the victims of racial terror lynchings. Their sister Ronda is also on the list.
