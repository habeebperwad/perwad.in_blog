James made his fortune through financial services business Raymond James, which was started by his father, Robert, in 1962. He stepped down as Raymond James' chairman in 2017, having served for 30 years. He was first made a board member at age 20. He is a graduate of Harvard College and Harvard Business School, where he graduated in the top 5% of his class. He is an avid art collector, with a particular interest in pieces from the American Southwest.
