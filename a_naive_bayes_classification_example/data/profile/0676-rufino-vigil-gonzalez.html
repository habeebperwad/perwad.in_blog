Rufino Vigil Gonzalez is a Mexican steel baron and chairman of specialty steel firm Industrias CH. The company has growth through acquisitions. In 1999 Industrias CH merged with steel manufacturer Grupo Ruvi and in 2001 it bought just under 83% of Grupo Simec, a steel smelter with factories in Guadalajara and Mexicali. Industrias CH now has 25 factories, primarily in Mexico, but also in the U.S.and Canada. It trades on the Mexican stock exchange. He owns nearly 67% of the company.
