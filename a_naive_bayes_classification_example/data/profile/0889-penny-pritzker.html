An heir to the Hyatt Hotels fortune, Penny Pritzker owns nearly 8% of Hyatt's stock. She was chair of credit-reporting firm TransUnion and cofounder of real estate investment firm Artemis Real Estate Partners. She is one of 11 billionaire members of the extended Pritzker family, which sold its controlling stake in Marmon Group to Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway for $4.5 billion in 2008. From 2013 to 2017, Pritzker served as the Obama Administration's Secretary of Commerce.
