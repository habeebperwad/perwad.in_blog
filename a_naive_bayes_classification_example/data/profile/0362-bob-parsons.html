Colorful founder of web hosting firm, GoDaddy, he and his company became known for their racy Super Bowl ads. Parsons sold a majority stake in 2011 to private equity investors KKR, Silver Lake and TCV. The firm went public in 2015 Parsons, who stepped down as executive chairman in 2014, now owns shopping malls, motorcycle dealerships, an ad agency, a lending firm and a golf club. He and his wife have given away a reported $90 million to charities. He credits his time in the Marine Corps in Vietnam, where he received a Purple Heart medal and combat action ribbon, as preparing him for business.
