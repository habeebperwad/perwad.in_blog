Bloomberg cofounded financial information and media company Bloomberg LP in 1981. His 88% stake is estimated to be worth $45 billion. He has donated more than $4 billion to gun control, climate change and other causes. He got his start on Wall Street in 1966 with an entry level job at investment bank Salomon Brothers. They fired him 15 years later. He owns at least six homes, including ones in Bermuda and London.
