Stephen Lansdown co-founded Hargreaves Lansdown, a British financial-services firm. The company manages nearly $70 billion in assets and boasts 876,000 clients. His co-founder Peter Hargreaves is a billionaire, too. Lansdown owns just under 20% of the firm. He also owns Bristol Sport, an entity overseeing the commercial operations of the Bristol City Football Club, Bristol Rugby Club, Bristol Academy Women's Football, Bristol Flyers Basketball and DZ Racing. Bristol Sport is also redeveloping Ashton Gate Stadium in Bristol. relocated in 2010 to Guernsey, a small island in the English Channel known for lenient tax laws.
