Antti Aarnio-Wihuri heads Wihuri Group, a Finnish conglomerate that has been in his family for over 100 years. The group, which employs 5,000 people, has interests in the packaging, technical trade, food wholesale and aviation sectors, and operates Caterpillar construction equipment dealerships, Metro daily goods wholesale stores and a private jet charter and ambulance service. He is the largest shareholder of Winpak, a Canadian company that specializes in producing packaging materials.
