A graduate of the Magnitogorsk Mining and Metallurgical Institute, Pyotr Kondrashev worked for 35 years at the Silvinit fertilizer factory. He became the firm's CEO in 1990 and later organized the privatization of the company. He sold his stake in Silvinit, Russia's second-largest producer of potassium fertilizer, in the summer of 2010 and these days keeps a low profile in Vienna, Austria. He enjoys adventure tourism and climbed Mount Kilimanjaro in 2009.
